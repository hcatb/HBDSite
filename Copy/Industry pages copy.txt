Copywriting for Embedded Finance
Copywriting for Embedded Lending and B2B Credit
Headline: Copy that stands out when finance is everywhere 
Embedded finance is a difficult two-step sale. First you have to sell the platform on the benefits of offering capital. Then you have to help them sell that capital to their merchants. The problem is that most SaaS platforms are tech companies, not banks. They often struggle to market lending products without sounding confusing or predatory to their users.
How I help 
Your copy needs to bridge the gap between your tech and the end user's needs. I can help you position capital as fuel for inventory and expansion, rather than just debt. Grow faster with messaging frameworks that allow non-financial platforms to offer financial products with confidence, ensuring you stay compliant with FCA rules while still driving adoption.

Copywriting for Accounting & TaxTech
B2B Copywriting for Accounting Tech and Treasury Management
Headline: Copy that works for the modern CFO 
Accountants and CFOs have a famous radar for nonsense. They are not looking for hype or disruption. They are looking for accuracy, efficiency and risk mitigation. The biggest mistake marketing teams make in this sector is trying to sound like a cool consumer brand. If you promise a revolution to a finance leader who just wants to survive tax season, they will ignore you.
How I help 
Make the right impression with copy that speaks peer-to-peer. I focus on the actual workflow benefits like integration, reconciliation and time saved. Build trust with messaging that understands the impact that financial workflows make to financial teams and position your software as a solution to cross-business challenges.
