Contextual Finance that Converts.

Platforms don't want to be banks, but they want banking revenue. Your copy needs to sell the value of embedded lending—retention, LTV—to the platform, and the ease of capital to the end merchant.

The Challenge

Why Copy Matters in Embedded Finance

Embedded finance is a two-sided sale. You're pitching to sophisticated platform teams (e-commerce, marketplaces, SaaS) who care about stickiness and revenue share. But you're also creating the end-user experience for merchants who need capital without friction.

The messaging must work at both levels: strategic for the partnership conversation, simple for the merchant dashboard. Most embedded finance companies struggle to balance these audiences.

I've worked with YouLend—the category leader—creating everything from impact reports to partner enablement materials. I understand the regulatory nuance and the commercial reality.
