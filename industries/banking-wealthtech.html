Why Copy Matters in Banking & WealthTech

Consumer trust is earned in milliseconds and lost in moments. Your prospects are comparing you against decades-old institutions with branch networks and regulatory heritage. Your copy must signal safety while showcasing innovation.

Consumer Duty has raised the bar further: every piece of copy must demonstrate you're acting in the customer's best interest. This isn't just compliance—it's competitive advantage for those who get it right.

I help banking and wealthtech companies find the tone that's both trustworthy and modern—building the emotional case for switching while handling regulatory scrutiny.
